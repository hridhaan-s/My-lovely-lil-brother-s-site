About Me

Welcome to My Space!

Hi! I'm Ritvik Sahay, a 10-year-old student and Grade 5 learner with a big passion for space, technology, and creativity. I'm currently part of the Bitbuzz Squad, where I work as a Content Advisor, helping create engaging and exciting news content.

I love space and technology. I enjoy observing stars and planets, and I've even captured photos of them using my brother's telescope.

I also enjoy building projects with my brother and exploring coding to create cool things.

I've written space adventure books including The Galaxy Escape, The Rescue, and Light Years Before Dinosaurs.

I love learning new things, building ideas, and creating fun projects. This website is a place where I share my work, my ideas, and my dreams.

One day, I want to reach the stars.

Thank you for visiting my space!
