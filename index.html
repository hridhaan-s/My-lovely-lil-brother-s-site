I have always been curious about how the world and the universe work, and I enjoy exploring new ideas that challenge me to think creatively.

One of my favourite hobbies is coding because it allows me to turn my ideas into real projects. Every website or program I build teaches me something new and helps me improve my problem-solving skills. I also love writing because it gives me the opportunity to create exciting stories and share my imagination with the world. I have written books such as The Galaxy Escape, The Rescue, and Light Years Before Dinosaurs. Writing has taught me how to express my ideas and inspire others through creativity.

So far, I have written more than 50 articles covering topics such as space, aviation, technology, and science. Working with Bitbuzz has helped me improve my writing, research, and teamwork skills while sharing interesting knowledge with readers around the world.

I created this website to share my journey, books, projects, achievements, and interests with others. As I continue learning, building, writing, and exploring, I will keep updating this website with new projects, discoveries, and milestones. Thank you for visiting my portfolio, and I hope you enjoy exploring my work as much as I enjoy creating it!
